# Gentle AI integration

Gentle AI can assist SDO, but it does not define SDO.

## Can assist with

- Operational Spec drafting
- Risk analysis
- Runbook preparation
- Supervised execution
- Validation
- Evidence summarization
- Review and archive preparation

## Does not replace

- Human approval
- Operational accountability
- Validation criteria
- Evidence from real systems
- Rollback decisions

## Design rule

SDO should integrate with Gentle AI skills, agents, registry, and memory when available, but must remain usable without Gentle AI.
